RENT

Located in West Vancouver with views of the scenic North Shore mountains, Hugo Ray Park is home to the West Vancouver Cricket Club and their clubhouse. The fields are used primarily for cricket in the summer with soccer being played in the winter months.The Pavilion is available for public rentals. Groups can host seminars and it is the perfect alternative for corporate meetings. Our downstairs board room can accommodate up to 25 people for a meeting or training session.

The upstairs bar and lounge is ideal for special occasions such as wedding receptions, birthday parties, anniversaries, cocktail receptions and various other private and promotional events. The seating capacity is 70 for a formal lunch or dinner and 90 for a reception style event. There is also a separate dance floor with facilities for a band or other similar entertainment.

The lounge boasts expansive windows with views of the grounds and mountains. The doors open up onto two large adjoining balconies with access to the grounds providing space for an extension to your indoor area on beautiful summer days! Ample free parking is available. The Clubhouse has a small kitchen with a stove and two refrigerators that can be used by caterers, depending on the event. We do not provide catering services but guests can hire their own caterer for their specific needs.

If you are interested in renting the facility, please contact us for further details and we will be happy to assist you with your arrangements. The clubhouse is easily accessible from Highway 1 and Taylor Way.
